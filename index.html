About Me

Nicolas R., known in the Perl community by his PAUSE ID "atoomic", is a software manager at WebPros with deep expertise in the Perl ecosystem. After directing development at Weborama from 2003 to 2011, he relocated to the United States to join cPanel, where he has been instrumental in advancing Perl toolchain development and CPAN infrastructure.

He is an active contributor to the Perl community, regularly presenting at major conferences and summits on topics including XS programming, dependency management, and memory profiling. His work spans from core Perl development to creating practical tools like grep.metacpan.org, and he maintains several open-source Perl modules including B::COW, Simple::Accessor, Clone, and Slack::WebHook.
