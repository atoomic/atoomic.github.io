## Overall Impression

A genuinely well-crafted, distinctive portfolio that mostly escapes the AI-template gravity well — then undercuts itself in three specific ways: a clipped mobile headline (mechanical P0), a hero that erases its own differentiator (strategic P0), and a contact path that can fail silently at the moment that matters most (P1). Fix those, strip the templated capabilities grid, and this moves from "Good" toward the "Excellent" band the craft clearly reaches for.

## What's Working

1. **The "High-Voltage Stage" is a real brand system, not a costume.** Gold as the single live wire, the ☢ trefoil with layered delight, dual radial glows, orbit-ring portrait. Design *is* the product, as the brand register demands. (`.brand-mark`, `@keyframes brand-energize|brand-spin|brand-flare`.)
2. **Typography hierarchy is genuinely crafted.** Manrope 800 on a fluid `clamp()` scale, tracking tightens with size, and `.text-gradient` is *correctly* restricted to display headings with a solid-`accent-strong` reduced-motion fallback. Compliant with the brand rule, not just present.
3. **Accessibility baseline is serious** — skip link, 3px gold `:focus-visible`, ARIA throughout, semantic landmarks, arrow-key nav, thorough `prefers-reduced-motion` block that force-reveals content, plus thoughtful email obfuscation.

## Priority Issues

### [P0] Mobile hero headline clips — "engineering." is truncated to "engineerin"
- **Why it matters:** Explicit impeccable ban (text overflowing its container) on the hero — the 10-second scan surface. At 390px, `clamp(3.2rem, 18vw, 5.7rem)` ≈ 70px; "engineering." (single un-breakable word) renders ~460px against a ~350px container and is cut by `.hero { overflow: clip }` + `body { overflow-x: hidden }`. Verified via CSS + math. A clipped headline reads as broken, not bold.
- **Fix:** Lower the mobile clamp ceiling (e.g. `clamp(2.6rem, 13vw, 5rem)`) and/or allow the word to wrap (`overflow-wrap: anywhere; hyphens: auto` on `.hero-title .line`). Verify at 320 / 360 / 390 / 430.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable typeset`

### [P0] Hero buries the differentiator (strategic miss vs. the brief)
- **Why it matters:** The brief's memorable line is *"Deep systems engineer. Now orchestrating AI agents,"* and "rare systems depth (Perl/XS/CPAN/memory profiling)" is belief-ladder step 2. The hero leads "Agentic engineering." with copy about agentic systems/workflows/platforms — zero systems-depth signal above the fold. A recruiter sees "another AI person," not "the rare engineer-leader who bridges the two." Biggest threat to "win the 10-second scan."
- **Fix:** Make the hero the bridge itself (e.g. *"Deep systems.* / *Now agentic."*) and put one systems-depth artifact above the fold — replace the "Agentic toolkit" floating card (which repeats the hero) with "CPAN author · B::COW · XS internals."
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable clarify`

### [P1] Light-theme gold text fails WCAG AA (2.37:1)
- **Why it matters:** `--accent` becomes `#c99900` on `--bg-light #f4f4ef` = **2.37:1** (fails both 4.5:1 body and 3:1 large-text). DESIGN.md §2 claims ≥4.5:1 — the math contradicts the doc. `var(--accent)` is used as text color in ~10 rules (eyebrows, `.accent-line`, links, `.company`, timeline rail). Every gold text element in light theme fails AA.
- **Fix:** Deepen light-theme `--accent` to a gold that clears 4.5:1 on `#f4f4ef` (target ≈ `#8a6b00`–`#7a5f00` territory, verify with a contrast check), or darken the light bg. Re-run contrast on every accent-as-text selector.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable colorize`

### [P1] Contact conversion is fragile at the highest-stakes moment
- **Why it matters:** Email is JS-gated ("Enable JavaScript to view email"); the form is a `mailto:` generator that sets status "Opening your email application…" and silently depends on a configured mail client. On locked-down corporate laptops / many mobile browsers (no mail client), the redirect does nothing and the status hangs forever — no timeout, no error, no "copy this address" fallback. A silent failure at the moment a recruiter decides to reach out costs the conversation the site exists to start.
- **Fix:** Show the decoded email as visible text (obfuscation only buys load-time scraper resistance); replace `mailto:`-only with a real endpoint (Formspree / Cloudflare Worker); at minimum, after the redirect show "If nothing opened, copy this address: …" with a copy button and a timeout fallback.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden`

### [P1] Templated scaffolding: eyebrow on EVERY section + numbered capability grid (two explicit bans)
- **Why it matters:** `.eyebrow` (uppercase, 0.16em tracked) repeats above all seven content sections — flattens hierarchy and reads as AI scaffolding (the detector's one finding lives here). The capabilities section then stacks a *second* ban: three identical `01/02/03` icon-cards. Both are explicit impeccable bans.
- **Fix:** Keep the eyebrow on ≤2–3 orienting sections (FAQ, Contact); let titles carry the rest. Break the capability grid's symmetry — feature card 01 larger with a real artifact, demote 02/03, drop the `01/02/03` markers (icons already differentiate). Keep "01 · About" only if it becomes a single deliberate flourish, not a system.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable quieter` (eyebrows) + `/impeccable distill` (capability grid)

## Minor Observations

- Two résumé links ("Resume" header + "Download my Resume" hero) both open `raw.githubusercontent.com` PDF in a viewer — `target="_blank"`, no `download` attribute. CTA promises "Download," behavior is "view."
- `role-type` labels drift: "Leadership / Architecture / AI / Mobile / Engineering" — eBoxr advisory tagged "Mobile" is odd for a strategic role.
- `.about-index` "01 · About" is an orphaned device matching nothing.
- Trust bar sits on expensive post-hero real estate with a tagline + 3 generic icons (adjacent to the "trusted-by logo strip" ban, saved only by using icons+labels not customer logos).
- Theme FOUC risk: `applyTheme` runs in JS (`script.js`), so first paint is dark even for light-preferring users. An inline head script setting `data-theme` before paint would fix it.
- `--text-muted #767f8e` on `#090b10` = 4.87:1 (passes 4.5 body but is the riskiest dark pair), and it's used on small text (`.fact-label` 0.72rem, `.floating-label` 0.68rem) — fine, but watch it.

## Questions to Consider

1. **Why does the hero hide the one thing that differentiates you?** The brief's memorable line is *"Deep systems engineer. Now orchestrating AI agents"* — but the hero reads "Agentic engineering." and erases the systems half. You're positioning into the most crowded lane (AI person) instead of the only lane you alone occupy (rare systems depth + AI). What's the smallest reliable endpoint that would make "send a note" actually land?

What is *one* quantified artifact (a module-download count, a team-size number, a "shipped X to Y users") you could put above the fold that proves depth without you having to say it?
